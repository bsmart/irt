The Halahan Boys

Lukas and Nathan are living life on bikes and their little brother Shane is tagging along too. Pedaling before the age of the 3, the now 11, 10, and 5 yo boys from Gibsonia, PA have big plans for their futures on bikes.

Lukas and Nathan have been busy traveling throughout the US and Canada riding, competing, shooting photos, and filming edits for the companies that support them. Having fun, making new friends, and riding some amazing places along the way. All the while creating memories to last a lifetime.

Let's ride bikes

Next Generation Youth Jam

The boys created Next Generation, a brand that promotes youth on bikes. This past March with parental, local, and support from their sponsors they held their first jam at The Wheel Mill Bike Park. The inaugural Next Generation Youth Jam was a huge success with nearly 300 youth age riders in attendance from as far away as Portland, OR. According to their father, Mike, the boys seem to enjoy the non riding aspects of BMX and MTB (building jumps and promoting bike riding) almost as much as the riding itself.

Pennsylvania Trails Advocacy Group

Welcome the Halahan family to PTAG stewardship. They will be focused on the North Park Freeride Skills Area. The boys will pull from all their resources and support to create a fun challenging atmosphere at the Skills Park. Jams, clinics, product demos and weekly rides are all on the list of things to come. So, when you see the pint sized rippers slinging clay or boosting the jumps introduce your self, the boys are always looking to make new riding friends!
